This is how it works

Managing private keys is something your bitcoin app will almost certainly need to do. From a private key (which is merely a number between 1 and 0xFFFF FFFF FFFF FFFF FFFF FFFF FFFF FFFE BAAE DCE6 AF48 A03B BFD2 5E8C D036 4140) you can get an associated public key. A bitcoin address is merely the hash of that public key.
